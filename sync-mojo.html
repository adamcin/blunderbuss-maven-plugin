blunderbuss:sync

Full name:

net.adamcin:blunderbuss-maven-plugin:0.1.0-SNAPSHOT:sync

Description:

Upload the local repository cache.

Attributes:

  • Executes as an aggregator plugin.
  • Requires that Maven runs in online mode.
  • Is NOT inherited by default in multi-project builds.
